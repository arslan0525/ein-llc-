Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about US & UK formations.

What is included in the USA LLC Formation package?
Our package includes the Articles of Organization, Registered Agent service for 1 year, Operating Agreement, and filing with the Secretary of State. We handle everything from start to finish.
How long does it take to get an EIN?
We guarantee your EIN Number in just 24 to 48 hours. We manage the entire communication with the IRS for you — fast, hassle-free, and 100% guaranteed.
Do I need to be in the USA or UK to open the company?
No, the entire process is 100% remote. You can launch your business and open your bank account from anywhere in the world.
Which US state is best for my LLC?
Wyoming is highly recommended for e-commerce and digital businesses due to low fees and privacy. Delaware is preferred for startups looking for VC funding.
